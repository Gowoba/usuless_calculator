I don't know why, but this calculator loves the Maracanã stadium. Therefore, all of its answers are a function of the total area of the Maracanã. It converts the answer you want into the percentage equivalent to the area. I don't know if it will ever have a proper purpose, but I'll let it dream.

For comparasion with the calculator's answers, this is the Maracanã. Its total size in square meters, on which the calculator is based, is 186.638m².
